Account

Why can't I create an account?
Registering is really simple; all you need is a valid email address and a working phone number. You will only be required to enter an email address and a password to create your Personal Area; adding a phone number to the account happens at a later stage.
If you are unable to register, you may be facing one of the following errors:
• Country of residence not found
• Email is already connected to an account
• Enter a valid email address
• Invalid password
How can I change my personal details?
1.Log in to your account
2.Settings
3.Modify information
*Some information is account locked be sure to contact our customer service for secure information.
How can I change my account's currency?
An existing account cannot be updated with a new currency base once it's already created.
If you wish to use a different currency, simply open a new account with the base currency of your choice. In order to do so, you will need to login into your existing account on the website and then click on "Add an Account".
Please make sure that the “Currency” and “Chosen Platform” fields match your new base currency choice.

Deposits and withdrawals

How long does it take to process deposits and withdrawals?
During normal working hours, withdrawals are usually processed within a few hours. If the withdrawal request is received outside working hours, it will be processed the next working day.
Bear in mind that once processed by us, the time taken for your withdrawal to reflect will depend on the payment method.
Card withdrawals can take around 5-7 working days and International Bank Transfers may take 3-5 business days depending on your bank. SEPA and local transfers usually reflect within the same business day, as do E-Wallets transfers.
Please note that although card deposits are processed immediately, this does not mean that funds have already been received in our bank account as the bank clearing procure usually takes some days. However, we credit your funds immediately in order to be able to trade instantly and protect open positions. Unlike deposits, the withdrawal procedure takes longer
